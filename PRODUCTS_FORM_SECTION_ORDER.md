# Products Form - Section Reorganization

## Ideal Order for Better UX:

### 1. Basic Information (Always visible)
- Product Name
- Slug
- Product Type
- Brand
- Short Description
- Description

### 2. Product Status (Collapsible - OPEN by default)
- Status (Draft/Active/Inactive)
- Availability (In Stock/Out of Stock/Pre-Order/Backorder)
- Visibility

### 3. Categories (Collapsible - OPEN by default)
- Primary Category
- Additional Categories

### 4. Tags (Collapsible - OPEN by default)
- Tag input

### 5. Pricing & Inventory (Collapsible - OPEN by default)
- Regular Price, Sale Price, Cost Price
- SKU, Barcode

### 6. Shipping & Dimensions (Collapsible - CLOSED by default)
- Requires Shipping
- Weight & Dimensions

### 7. Product Attributes (Collapsible - CLOSED by default)
- Attribute selection

### 8. Product Videos (Collapsible - CLOSED by default)
- Video management

### 9. Collections (Collapsible - CLOSED by default)
- Collection selection

### 10. Related Products (Collapsible - CLOSED by default)
- Related product selection

### 11. Product Bundles (Collapsible - CLOSED by default)
- Bundle management

### 12. FAQs (Collapsible - CLOSED by default)
- FAQ management

### 13. Product Flags (Collapsible - CLOSED by default)
- Featured, New Arrival, Best Seller, On Sale

### 14. Scheduling & Dates (Collapsible - CLOSED by default)
- Publish Date, Expiry Date

### 15. Custom Fields (Collapsible - CLOSED by default)
- Custom key-value pairs

---

## Rationale:

**Top Priority (Open by default):**
- Status, Categories, Tags, Pricing - Most frequently used fields

**Medium Priority (Closed but accessible):**
- Shipping, Attributes, Videos, Collections, Related Products, Bundles, FAQs

**Low Priority (Closed, rarely used):**
- Flags, Scheduling, Custom Fields

This order follows the natural workflow of adding a product.
